⭐ Star Trek 3D Chess Rules:
Three main boards stacked vertically (Levels 1–5), plus four 2×2 Attack Boards (QL1, KL1 for White; QL5, KL5 for Black). Standard chess movement applies within each level. Pieces may cross levels via the center transfer squares (↕ d4/e4). Attack Board movement: A player may spend their entire turn to move an Attack Board to an adjacent anchor point, provided it has at most one piece of their own color and no enemy pieces. Pieces on an Attack Board maintain their position when the board moves. Kings may not cross levels. Pieces on higher levels block movement through the same (x,y) column.

Controls: Click "Move Atk Board" button to enter board-move mode, then click an Attack Board to select it and click a valid anchor point to move it. Toggle off to return to piece-move mode.
