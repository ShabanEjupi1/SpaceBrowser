ℹ️ About Çiftelia
The Çiftelia (çif·tel·ia) is a traditional two-stringed Albanian instrument from the mountains of Gegënia — the northern highlands.

It has one melody string (played with frets) and one drone string (open, continuous). The drone creates the characteristic buzzing resonance.

Played at weddings, vallëzimi (folk dances) and epic singing (kënget e kreshnikëve).
